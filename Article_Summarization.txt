The Text Summarization is creating a summary of the given Text.
Here I have taken Text= News Article
The program fetches the data from web using BeautifulSoup package of python.
Tokenization , stopword removal functions are used.
Then frequency count of each word is caluculated, the frequency is normalized in the range of 0-1. Outlier words , which are not in the range are removed and summary is generated.
The result is quite meaningful and can conclude as summary of the Article.
